Secret Idioms Jam

Itch.io Link

Kokolo Game/Art Collective is proud to announce our second game jam, taking place once again at Cyber Love Garden. This month’s jam is open to any and all budding or veteran game developers, and contrary to our first event, we are allowing the use of any game-making tool. For this jam’s theme, we have curated a list of common idioms (aka turns of phrase), and will allow participants to claim them as their own personal “themes” for the jam. This list will only become public after the event has started, and will be available on a first-come-first-served basis. The jam will kick-off on the 20th of October at 3pm. Participants will have a week to develop their projects, and we’ll have a showcase on the 27th. Afterward, we plan to curate and publish this collection of works on our Itch page, to further our goals of community development and public-facing game packages. Participation in this post-jam publishing process is of course not mandatory. Please contact us via email at kokolo-game-art-collective@proton.me or on Instagram @kokolo_collective for more information or to receive the Cyber Love Garden address.
